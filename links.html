This is a simple program to make counting and tabulating money received easy.

This is a program for gathering and generating the data for the Annual Survey of Fraternal Activity (1728). After completing the 10784 (see above), you can enter the data into the program. At the end of the year, the Financial Secretary or Program Director can click 'generate annual report' and transfer the provided numbers onto the form for submission to supreme. The user can also view and edit the entries as needed.
